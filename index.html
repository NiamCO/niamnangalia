About Me

Young Actor & Performer

Hi, I'm Niam Nangalia, a 13-year-old actor from Washington with a huge passion for storytelling. I've been insanely inspired by shows like "Stranger Things" and seeing young actors my age bring incredible characters to life on screen.

I already have acting experience through creating original short films with my brother, where we write, film, and star in our own projects. This hands-on practice has taught me about character development, on-screen chemistry, and the filmmaking process.

My forever dream is to be part of a professional TV show, movie, or any production where I can contribute my energy and dedication. I'm excited, ready to work hard, and looking for opportunities to grow as a young performer.
